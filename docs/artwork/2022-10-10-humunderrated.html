One of the first of the many drawings of Nira’s design from Zutomayo’s Humanoid MV I’ll end up making for quite a long while.

I don’t think the redrawn bit on the left of her visor could’ve been any more noticeable.

Besides that nitpick, I’m happy with this one - especially with the hair! I feel like it conveys pretty well its… volume? Although, Nira’s hair in the MV isn’t nearly this “fluffy” looking, due to Sakiyama (its artist)’s really crude artstyle. At first, I can remember really disliking it - then it suddenly started growing on me: her style fits both the MV’s imagery (perhaps unsurprisingly?) and the song’s chaotic, yet incredible instrumental track like a glove.

One of my first attempts at drawing the Nira design from the Only Radiant DNA MV.

I think the little depth added by the lighting on this one really made a noticeable difference, the clothing looks pretty decent too. Overall, I think it’s pretty cool (+ I ended up using it as my profile picture in a bunch of places).

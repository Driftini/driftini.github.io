Time Left

21 Oct 2022

no label

Open original

Quick doodle of Nira from Zutomayo’s new Time Left music video, mostly brush experimentation. The background objectively sucks.

I’ve also made the lineart a bit thicker in some areas to simulate the kind of “dynamic” strokes you’d get out of a drawing tablet.

I strongly disliked this when I first made it, and ended up going on an art break for over a week, suspecting I was just burnt out. I eventually started drawing again when I bought a drawing tablet a couple weeks later. Now I’m a little less disgusted by it.

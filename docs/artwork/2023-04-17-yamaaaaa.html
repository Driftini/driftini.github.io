Yama, somehow

17 Apr 2023

no label

Open original

I couldn’t contain my love for Yama’s music anymore and finally drew her.

Don’t be fooled by the color and shading - this is not a high-effort drawing, the lineart shows that very well. Also, most heinous of digital art crimes, I used the Transform tool to scale and ROTATE(!) the lineart, that’s why it looks somewhat blurry. I’m absolutely deserving of a public execution now.

I kinda regret not making the warm tint on the shadows a bit stronger: they’re a bit too pale, I think.

Be warned that this isn’t the last you’ll see of Yama from me… faaar from that.

Artwork Archive

As you may have guessed from my about page, drawing is pretty much my biggest hobby.
This page contains the vast majority of my sketches, high effort drawings, digital paintings... you name it.
Why? Well, it's both for myself, to see how I'm improving over the years, and for other people to have a single, tidy page containing all of my artwork.

Why not use something like DeviantArt or Pixiv, I hear you ask? Making my own archive is just more fun, gives me more control over how my works are presented and more independence overall.
Besides, I have my own gripes with both sites' userbases, so not having to directly interact with them is an added bonus.

That aside, I do share new posts on whichever social media site I happen to be using at the moment, which now would be Wet-Dry World.
